• You are sharing a link to an album: Upon accepting the invitation, the invited people will get updates in Neebla whenever the album gets updated.
  • Number of people to invite: The sharing invitation will be sent using email (just tap on "Invite" below to add email addresses). You can send this to this many people via email or post to a social media channel, and this many people will be able to redeem it.
  • Permissions
    • Read-only: The invited person can only download images & discussions. They don't get to add new images or make new comments.
    • Read & Write: The invited person can also add new images and make new comments.
    • Read, Write, & Invite: The invited person can also invite other people to the share the album.
  • Allow social acceptance? If you allow this, then Facebook user(s) can accept the invitation. If the permissions you give allow them to add new images, then these image and discussion files will be stored in your cloud storage (or the original owning user's cloud storage). The default does not allow social acceptance.
  • Expiry: Invitations expire after 24 hours.
